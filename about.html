About

The Novel of Comedy (TNoC)

A streaming-like atlas and critical cartography of humorous fiction — built for stable comparison across works: Comedy of Society, Comic Crime, Neo-Nonsense.

Created & curated by Boris Jeanson Béteille · Back to the atlas

Credits

The Novel of Comedy (TNoC) is an independent editorial project. Editorial accountability, selection, writing and taxonomy are curated by Boris Jeanson Béteille.

If you reference or quote TNoC, please cite the project name and link to the source page.

Method (short)

TNoC uses stable templates (including a fixed 10-part novel guide matrix) to make comic mechanisms comparable across books and authors.

More details live on the Method page: method.html.

How to cite TNoC

Copy/paste (web):

The Novel of Comedy (TNoC), “<Title of page>”, https://www.thenovelofcomedy.com/<page> (accessed <YYYY-MM-DD>).

Short credit line (for slides, notes, posts):

Source: The Novel of Comedy (TNoC) — https://www.thenovelofcomedy.com/
