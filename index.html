Guides

Novel, author and series guides

The heart of TNoC is made of guides. Currents, branches, environments and Labs are there to illuminate them and connect them to one another.

Novel guides
The novel guide is the basic unit of The Novel of Comedy: a fixed ten-chapter matrix, identical from one book to the next.
Author profiles
Author profiles gather what concerns the way an author works with humour: through-lines in a career, recurring motifs, relation to language and society.
Long-running series guides
Some comedies only fully unfold over time: academic cycles, comic crime series, large family sagas.
