Currents

Three currents of modern humorous fiction

A genealogy as well as a map: social satire first (Comedy of Society), then comic crime (Crime Comedy), and finally absurdist / postmodern comedy (Neo-Nonsense).

This page keeps it short. Full definitions, branches and examples: on the Currents page.

Comedy of Society Map →

Social satire & comedy of manners: family, work, hierarchy, class.

Signature: institutions generate the comic engine.

Crime Comedy Map →

Comic crime fiction: capers, heists, schemes, elastic morality.

Signature: transgression becomes a narrative machine.

Neo-Nonsense Map →

Absurdist & postmodern comic fiction: rigorous logic in a cracked world.

Signature: meaning wobbles — the mechanism stays precise.
