Guides

Novel, author and series guides

The heart of TNoC is made of guides. Currents, branches, environments and Labs are there to illuminate them and connect them to one another.

Novel guides

The novel guide is the basic unit of The Novel of Comedy: a fixed ten-chapter matrix, identical from one book to the next.

  • Reading note – a signed note;
  • Incipit – opening lines;
  • Characters – archetypes and line-citations;
  • Cartography – current, branch, environment, Lab;
  • Legacy – reception and posterity.
Author profiles

Author profiles gather what concerns the way an author works with humour: through-lines, motifs, relation to language and society.

Some comedies unfold over time: cycles, series, sagas.
